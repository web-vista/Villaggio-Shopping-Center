Villaggio Shopping Center

Welcome to Villaggio Shopping Center, your ultimate destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment in the heart of Fresno, California. We offer a diverse range of stores, from top international brands to local gems, all under one roof. Beyond shopping, enjoy a variety of dining options, a state-of-the-art cinema, and family-friendly amenities. Whether you're here for a quick visit or a full day of fun, we have something for everyone. More than just a mall, Villaggio Shopping Center is a community hub where you can shop, relax, and experience something new with every visit.
